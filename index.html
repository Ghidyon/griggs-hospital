Our Services

We maintain affiliations and/or referral relationships with facilities in the United States of America. Griggs Specialist Hospital, Lagos is affiliated with the Houston Texas, Obstetrics and Gynecology (OB/GYN) clinic of Kanayo E. Ubesie MD, PA., a US based S Corporation, registered in the State of Texas, USA. The practice established a Foreign Transfer Patient Program, which accepts transfer patients from all over the world, who seek US level of health care.

Prenatal care, delivery and/or post-partum care

Patients enrolled in the program may choose and register for the delivery of their baby at any of the two modern Labor and Delivery Centers in Houston, Texas, USA. The facilities are at:

  • Memorial Hermann Hospital Southwest, Houston, Texas and
  • HCA West Hospital, Houston, Texas.

For more information on the prenatal transfer program, please contact us on +2347066050690. You can directly call the sister practice location in Houston, on 7137731700 or by email, on drkubesieobgyn@gmail.com.

In each case, ask for the Prospectus for Foreign Transfer Patient Document and provide us with your email address for the document to be sent to you.

Fertility Services

Griggs Specialist Hospital partners with Houston Fertility Institute, Houston, Texas USA to provide you with cutting-edge services in fertility medicine. Through a program designed to suit you specifically, your goal of having a baby will be achieved. Services available include:

  1. Ovulation induction
  2. Timed Intercourse
  3. Intra Uterine Insemination (IUI)
  4. In Vitro Fertilization (IVF)
  5. Intra-Cytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI)
  6. Pre-Implantation Genetic Diagnosis PGD)
  7. Sex Selection
  8. Surrogacy
  9. Embryo Cryopreservation/Banking
  10. Egg Retrieval, Donation and Cryopreservation/Banking
  11. Sperm Donation and Cryopreservation/Banking
Cancer Diagnosis and Treatment Referral Services

Patients are referred to The University of Texas, MD Anderson Cancer Center, Houston, Texas USA. The University of Texas, MD Anderson Cancer Center is one of the original three comprehensive cancer centers in the United States of America, established by the US National Cancer Act of 1971.

The center and its multiple affiliates still maintain the top position in the world, with regards to cancer diagnosis, treatment, technology and innovation. Please visit the MD Anderson Cancer Center website.

Other reputable oncology centers, such as Texas Oncology Centers, expand thereferral base and choices available to our patients.

Advanced Medical and Surgical Referral

Griggs Specialist Hospital maintains a database of US based referral doctors in all medical/surgical specialties. These include:

  • Nephrology (dialysis)
  • Internal medicine (diabetes, hypertension)
  • Infectious disease(HIV/AIDS), Cardiology (heart)
  • Urology (prostate)
  • Oncology (cancer), Vascular surgery (aneurysm)
  • Neurosurgery (brain)
  • Orthopedic (bone, joint, spine)
  • Bariatric surgery (weight loss)
  • Plastic surgery and Burn/Wound care surgery
